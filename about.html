About Us

Wellness, as a mindful journey.

Pure Passion Yoga is a space to move, breathe, and grow — with on-demand classes, guided challenges, a living pose library, and the tools to build a practice that's genuinely yours.

Browse classes Start a challenge

Welcome to Pure Passion Yoga

We see wellness as more than a routine. It's an ongoing practice of balance, self-discovery, and living with intention — one breath, one pose, one small choice at a time.

Whether you're rolling out your mat for the very first time or deepening a practice you've loved for years, everything here is designed to meet you where you are — with warmth, and without pressure.

What you'll find here

A whole practice, in one place

From your first gentle stretch to planning a class of your own, Pure Passion Yoga grows with you.

Online Classes

On-demand yoga to practice anytime — from restorative and gentle to energizing flow — guided by instructors who feel like they're right there with you.

Challenges

Guided, multi-day journeys that open a new practice each day — so you build a steady rhythm and actually feel your progress unfold.

Pose Library

A growing, illustrated library of postures with Sanskrit names, clear cues, and modifications — a reference you'll return to again and again.

Sequence Builder

For teachers and curious practitioners: shape your own flows pose by pose, then take them to the mat — or to your class.

Yoga Shop

Journals, coloring books, and mindful tools to carry your practice off the mat and into the small, everyday moments.

Our Story

Founded from a love of the practice

Pure Passion Yoga began with a simple love of yoga and mindful living. Its founder, Kristine Koller — a Registered Yoga Teacher based in Las Vegas — has walked the path of practice since 2020, and what started as a personal passion grew into a place to share it.

We built this platform because wellness isn't one-size-fits-all. It looks a little different for every body and every season of life, and there's real beauty in practices that honor individuality over perfection.

"Come as you are — the practice will meet you there."

Kristine Koller · Founder & Teacher

RYT-200 Las Vegas Practicing since 2020

Our Philosophy

What we believe

  • Yoga is for every body and every stage of life.
  • Mindfulness belongs in ordinary moments — not just on the mat.
  • Creativity is a genuine form of self-care, worth making time for.
  • Growth comes through compassion, not perfection.
  • Small, intentional practices add up to meaningful change.

A community rooted in mindfulness

More than a library of classes — a place to grow, together

We're committed to keeping this space welcoming, inclusive, and honest: one that empowers you rather than pressures you. Wherever you are in your journey, we're grateful to be part of it.
